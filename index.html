Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We’ve answered the most common questions about setting up, running, and maintaining a Bloop gaming station in your venue.

How long does it take to install a Bloop station?

Most installations take less than one hour. All hardware and software is preconfigured before we arrive — we just plug in, test, and you’re live.

What kind of games are included?

We license a curated selection of multiplayer games through our Steam partnership, including titles like Overcooked, Tricky Towers, and more — perfect for cafés, couples, and casual players.

How do customers pay to play?

Players tap their card on the kiosk’s contactless reader to start a timed session. Pricing is fully customizable and all payments are processed securely.

Does Bloop require internet?

Yes, each Bloop station needs a stable internet connection for license verification, software updates, and remote monitoring.

Can I customize the games or layout?

Yes — we offer modular options based on your space and audience. Games, session length, pricing, and visual overlays can all be tailored.

Is support and maintenance included?

Absolutely. We monitor each unit remotely and provide technical support, game updates, and content management so you don’t have to worry.
